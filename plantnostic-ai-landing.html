Q: Why are my monstera leaves turning yellow?

Yellowing leaves (chlorosis) can be caused by:

  • Overwatering — Most common. Check soil moisture.
  • Underwatering — Leaves wilt then yellow.
  • Low light — Reduce chlorophyll production.
  • Nutrient deficiency — Nitrogen, magnesium, or iron.
  • Pests — Check undersides of leaves.

💡 Pro tip: Let top 2-3 inches of soil dry before watering.
